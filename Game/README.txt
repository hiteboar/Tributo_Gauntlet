Description:
	- This is a tribute to Arcade Gauntlet, in it you will find only one level with some enemy spawn points. 
	  There are 5 diferent types of enemeyes:
		Ghost: Comes to the player and dies when collides with him
		Grunt: Goes to a near position from the player and attacks him
		Sorcerer: Similar to Grunts but he can turns himself invisible for a moment being immortal in that state
		Lobber: Keeps the distance between him and the player and throws stones, this stones can go through walls and enemies
		Demon: Similar to Lobbers but shots fire balls that can't go through walls and can hurt other enemies

	  In order to complete the level, the player must destroy all the enemy spawn points and all the enemies in the level. 
	  
	  For every coin you put in the game, the player health increases in 750 points

	- Controls:
		- W, A, S, D --> move the character
		- ENTER --> Shot
		- SPACE BAR --> insert coin
		- LEFT CONTROL --> Start
